Foundation of all competitive exam preparation.

HSC BOARD

Covers the complete 11th & 12th science curriculum. Mastering HSC sets a strong base for all entrance exams and opens doors to multiple career paths.

MHT-CET

Maharashtra Common Entrance Test

State-level exam for Engineering & Pharmacy.

MHT-CET

Gateway to top engineering and pharmacy colleges across Maharashtra including VJTI, COEP, ICT and hundreds more. Your ticket to premium education.

JEE

Joint Entrance Examination

National exam for IITs, NITs, and IIITs.

JEE MAINS & ADVANCED

The most prestigious engineering entrance in India. Crack JEE to unlock IIT Bombay, Delhi, Kharagpur and the highest-paying engineering careers.

NEET

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test

Gateway to MBBS, BDS, Nursing & Pharmacy.

NEET UG

India's single medical entrance exam. Score high to secure MBBS at AIIMS New Delhi, top government colleges, and build a life-saving medical career.
